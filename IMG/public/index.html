Our Crewmates

The crew consists of several members, each with their unique abilities and personalities, including Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Robin, Franky, and Brook.Zoro is a skilled swordsman who values strength and honor, while Nami is a cunning navigator with a passion for money. Together, they travel the Grand Line, fighting foes and searching for the ultimate treasure, the One Piece.

Our Territories

The current Territories of the crew are Fishman Island, The land of Wano and unlike the unpopular belief Alabasta and Dressrosa can be considered their Territories. The Crew got the Fishman Island from the Yonko Big Mom and they raided the Land of Wano to free the land from the reign of the Yonko Kaido who is dubbed as the Strongest Creature Alive.

Our Allies

Our Allies are Trafargar D Water Law, Captain Kidd, Red haired Shanks, Sun Pirates, Strawhat Grand Fleet which consists of more than 5000 pirates. Moreover, Yamato-The Son of kaido, Reminents of the Whitebeard Pirates, The Revolutionary Army can be considered our Allies
